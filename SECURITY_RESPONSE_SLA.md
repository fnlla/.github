# Security Response SLA

## Scope

This SLA applies to public FNLLA repositories and complements `SECURITY.md`.

## Severity Targets

- **Critical**
  - Acknowledge: within 1 business day
  - Initial triage: within 2 business days
  - Mitigation plan: within 3 business days
- **High**
  - Acknowledge: within 2 business days
  - Initial triage: within 5 business days
  - Mitigation plan: within 7 business days
- **Medium / Low**
  - Acknowledge: within 3 business days
  - Initial triage: within 10 business days
  - Mitigation plan: scheduled by risk and release cadence

## Process

1. Report privately to `security@fnlla.co.uk`.
2. Include repository, version, impact, and reproduction.
3. FNLLA security triage classifies severity and confirms handling plan.
4. Coordinated disclosure is used by default.

## Exceptions

Large ecosystem-wide refactors or third-party vendor delays can extend timelines;
status updates are still provided during remediation.
